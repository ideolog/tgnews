LAST SEASON

The Baggies averaged 24,198 in 2018/2019 - which placed them 9th in the Championship, behind Leeds United, Aston Villa, Nottingham Forest, Derby, Stoke, Norwich and both Sheffield clubs.

CAPACITY

The Hawthorns has a capacity of 26,688 according to the club website. They came closest to filling it last season against Sheffield Wednesday on 29 December when 26,548 watched a 1-1 draw.

The lowest was when Swansea came in March 2019, when their 3-0 win was played out in front of just 20,282.
