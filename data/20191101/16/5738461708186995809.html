FSAI

Louth takeaway hit with Closure Order

Food Safety Authority of Ireland

01 Nov 2019, 16:44 by David Lynch

The Food Safety Authority of Ireland (FSAI) have served a Closure Order on Glyde Fries Takeaway on Main Street in Tallanstown, Co Louth.

The order was served on October 25 to Anway Ahmadi.

An Improvement Order under the FSAI Act, 1998, had previously been served on October 8, but was lifted on October 22.
