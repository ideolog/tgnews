Abortion

01 Nov 2019, 16:04 by BRIEF

CHICAGO — The Seventh Circuit denied the Indiana health commissioner’s request for a rehearing on a statute, which has yet to take effect, that forces minors to inform their parents that they plan to have an abortion.
