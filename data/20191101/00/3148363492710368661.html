John McLaughlin on Impeachment Probe: 'Thank God for the Deep State'

Appearing Wednesday at the “2020 Vision: Intelligence and the U.S. Presidential Election” event hosted by the Michael V. Hayden Center, former CIA Acting Director John McLaughlin lauded the “Deep State” for its role in the House Democrats’ partisan impeachment inquiry against President Donald Trump.
