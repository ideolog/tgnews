Suburban train discussion with Centre on November 4

01 Nov 2019, 00:30 by Bangalore Mirror Bureau

After PC Mohan, Bengaluru Central MP on Thursday met Railway Minister Piyush Goyal and Minister of State for Railways Suresh Angadi to expedite the suburban rails project, it has been decided that a meeting will be held in New Delhi on November 4 to discuss the project. Post the meeting, Suresh Angadi and the Railway Board Chairman will arrive in Bengaluru on November 5th to discuss the project with the State Government.

During the upcoming parliamentary session, all MPs from Karnataka will meet the Prime Minister to discuss Suburban railway implementation.
