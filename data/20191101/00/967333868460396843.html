Trump Nominates N. Korea Envoy Biegun to Be Next Deputy State Secretary - White House

WASHINGTON (Sputnik) - President Donald Trump has nominated US Special Representative for North Korea Stephen Biegun to be the next Deputy Secretary of State, the White House said in a press release.

01 Nov 2019, 00:42
"Today, President Donald J. Trump announced his intent to nominate the following individual to a key position in his Administration: Stephen E. Biegun of Michigan, to be Deputy Secretary of State", the release said on Thursday.
