Kartik Aaryan shares a new poster of 'Pati Patni Aur Woh'; introduces fans to the world of Chintu Tyagi

01 Nov 2019, 09:09 by TIMESOFINDIA.COM

Kartik Aaryan has come a long way in his career and established himself as one of the most talented actors of today's generation. He is currently grabbing all the attention for his looks, relationship status and professional commitments.

Taking to Instagram, Kartik shared a new poster of his upcoming film 'Pati Patni Aur Woh' introducing his fans to the world of his quirky character Chintu Tyagi. He captioned this poster as "#ChintuTyagi aaaaaaa rahe hain !! #PatiPatniAurWoh 👫💃🏻"

Not only that but he also announced that the film is slated to release on 6th December this year. The film which also features Ananya Panday and Bhumi Pednekar is being helmed by Mudassar Aziz.

Meanwhile, Kartik and Ananya have been spending a lot of time together recently and reports of them coming together after the actor's breakup with Sara Ali Khan have been making rounds on the internet.
