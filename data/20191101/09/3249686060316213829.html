Jessi drops MV for 'Drip' feat. Jay Park

01 Nov 2019, 09:05 by germainej

Jessi has dropped her music video for "Drip" featuring Jay Park.

The two rappers have come together for the fresh collaboration track composed by Jessi, johnjohn, and CuzD and written by Jessi, Jay Park, and johnjohn. "Drip" is also the second release after Jessi signed with P-Nation early this year.
