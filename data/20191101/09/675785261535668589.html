Eighteen-month-old boy dies after falling from block of flats in Tottenham

The Met Police are investigating after a baby boy died after plunging from a block of flats in Tottenham High Road in North London

01 Nov 2019, 09:55 by Talia Shadwell, William Janes, https://www.mirror.co.uk/authors/talia-shadwell/

An 18-month old baby boy has died after falling from a block of flats in Tottenham, north London.

The Met Police were called to the flats yesterday morning and found the boy in critical condition. The force today confirmed he was pronounced dead shortly after arriving in hospital.

Police and paramedics raced to the scene shortly before 10am yesterday.

Firefighters also attended and an air ambulance was scrambled to Tottenham High Road .

The baby was found in a "critical condition" and rushed to a north London hospital, but was pronounced dead shortly after arrival at around 11.40am.
