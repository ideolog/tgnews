Cyclone Maha over 1,000 km away from Oman

01 Nov 2019, 09:39 by Times News Service

Muscat: Tropical storm Maha is currently around 1,500 km away from the Sultanate's coast the Public Authority for Civil Aviation (PACA) announced.

PACA said in a statement online: "The wind speed around the center of the tropical storm Maha is (35-45) knots and it is still classified as a tropical storm with the continued movement in the north / northwest direction. It is currently 1,500 km away off the coast of the Sultanate and no effects are expected in the next five days."
