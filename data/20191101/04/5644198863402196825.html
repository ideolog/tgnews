Pinterest falls short of quarterly revenue estimates

01 Nov 2019, 04:15

Pinterest Inc on Thursday fell short of Wall Street estimates for third-quarter revenue, sending its shares down 20per cent in extended trading.

REUTERS: Pinterest Inc on Thursday fell short of Wall Street estimates for third-quarter revenue, sending its shares down 20per cent in extended trading.

Total revenue rose about 47per cent to US$279.7 million, but was below analysts' average estimate of US$280.6 million, according to IBES data from Refinitiv.
