In Case You Forgot, These Are The Major Nintendo Switch Titles This Holiday Season

Something for everyone

01 Nov 2019, 04:15 by Liam Doolan

Yesterday, Nintendo reconfirmed multiple upcoming releases on Nintendo Switch. Highly anticipated ones like Animal Crossing: New Horizons obviously won't be showing up until next year and Metroid Prime 4 doesn't even have a date.

Luckily, there are plenty of other first-party games ready to go this holiday season. The major ones in particular – as can be seen in the slide above – include Ring Fit Adventure, Luigi's Mansion 3 and Pokémon Sword and Shield.

Company president Shuntaro Furukawa also notes how there are plenty of other third-party options, to satisfy the "diverse needs" of consumers.

Other software publishers are also releasing titles in a variety of genres for the holiday season and onwards. What you see here is just a sampling, that includes titles that satisfy game fans and casual titles that can be enjoyed by many players together, revivals of past classics and indie titles created by developers with their own unique style, we have gathered a collection of various titles on Nintendo Switch to answer the diverse needs of consumers.
