One more freighter beached by MATMO

01 Nov 2019, 05:05 by Mikhail Voytenko

General cargo ship QUANG VINH 09 was beached by typhoon MATMO at Quy Nhon coast, central Vietnam, in the evening Oct 30, along with 5 other ships, reported by MB yesterday Oct 31.
General cargo ship QUANG VINH 09, IMO 8736318, dwt 3073, built 2008, flag Vietnam, manager XUAN LOC TRANSPORT CO LTD.
