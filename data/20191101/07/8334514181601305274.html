Yemen's Houthis down U.S.-made drone near Saudi border: spokesman

01 Nov 2019, 07:40

DUBAI (Reuters) - Yemen’s Houthi movement said on Friday its air defenses downed a U.S.-made ScanEagle drone near the Saudi border, the group’s military spokesman said.

The drone was gathering intelligence, Yahya Sarea, spokesman for the Houthi military, said in a Twitter post.
