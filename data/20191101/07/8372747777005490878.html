How to get 'HAMILTON' tickets in SWFL

01 Nov 2019, 07:05

LEE COUNTY, Fla.-- 'HAMILTON' tickets go on sale Saturday, November 2nd, 2019 at 8:00 A.M. You can either purchase tickets online or in person at the Barbara B. Mann Performing Arts Hall. You can only purchase four tickets.

Online ordering:

  • Click here to go to the website.
  • You cannot log in any earlier than 7 A.M. on Saturday, November 2nd, 2019.
  • After 8:00 A.M. you will be put into a waiting room.
  • You can choose your top 3 shows and price levels.
  • You cannot choose your seats.
  • Once you get your tickets, you will have 8 minutes to finish the sale.

In person ordering:

  • Starting at 6:00 A.M. until 7:30 A.M. numbered wristbands will be given to those at the Barbara B. Mann Performing Arts Hall box office, located at 13350 FSW Parkway, Fort Myers.
  • The numbers are random and dictate the order you will be called.
  • The wristband does not guarantee a ticket, just a place in line.

'HAMILTON' will be at the Barbara B. Mann Performing Arts Hall from January 14 - 26, 2020.
