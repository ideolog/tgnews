Colombian singer Fatiniza to perform at The FRDG Fringe in Dubai

And she’ll be performing alongside some of Dubai’s favourite local musicians

01 Nov 2019, 07:15 by Reema Rahman

Dubai is full of musical talent and since 2010 Colombian pop-fusion singer and songwriter Fatiniza has been making waves across the local music scene here in the UAE, helping pave the way for female artists in the region.

And after spending some time in Latin America recording new music and performing all over the world, she’s back in the UAE, where she previously lived, and set to showcase her new material at Dubai's The Fridge in Alserkal Avenue on Monday November 4.

Tickets for The FRDG Fringe start at Dhs30 – click here to nab yours today.

Some of Dubai’s favourite local musicians will be performing alongside Fatiniza so it’s set to be a top – and pocket-friendly – night out for fans of live music.
