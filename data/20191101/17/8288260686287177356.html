West Bromwich Albion latest - The Baggies are desperately hoping to keep hold of their star loanee Grady Diangana in the January window.

01 Nov 2019, 17:49 by Jamie Kemble

West Bromwich Albion could be set to lose star loanee Grady Diangana in the January transfer window.

That's according to Football Insider , who claim West Ham United will recall the winger who has racked up four goals and three assists in his first 12 games with Albion .

The forward has wasted no time winning over fans at The Hawthorns, helping his loan club to the top of the Championship table.

But just like when Harvey Barnes was recalled by Leicester in January of last season, West Brom 's promotion hopes could be dealt a significant blow.

Football Insider claim West Ham are planning on recalling Diangana in the winter window..
