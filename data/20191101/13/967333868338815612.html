Four People Reportedly Killed, 10 Wounded at Halloween Party Shooting in California - Video

The shooting happened at a home on Lucille Way near Knickerbocker Lane in Orinda, California.

01 Nov 2019, 13:40

At least four people have been killed and 10 more injured at a Halloween party in Orinda, California, overnight, local media reports say.

According to police officials, an investigation is underway.
