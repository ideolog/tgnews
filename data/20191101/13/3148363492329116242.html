Bill DeBlasio Cheers Donald Trump's Move to Florida

New York City Mayor Bill de Blasio cheered the news Thursday that President Donald Trump has decided to move his permanent residence to Florida.

01 Nov 2019, 13:11 by Charlie Spiering
