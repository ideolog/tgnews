JUI-F holds Azadi March rally in Islamabad

01 Nov 2019, 15:58 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Prominent leaders from opposition parties have joined Jamiat Ulema-e-Islam-Fazl (JUI-F) on Friday for Azadi March rally in federal Capital, Islamabad.  

Addressing the rally, Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz (PML-N) President and Opposition Leader in National Assembly (NA) Shehbaz Sharif said that time has come to topple the ‘fake government’ of Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI). The ‘selected PM’ has destroyed the economy of Pakistan and students are crying for the scholarships, he continued.

The PML-N leader said that inflation was at low level during ex-PM Nawaz Sharif’s tenure and that the nation is cursing this incumbent government of PTI for not providing them promised relief.

Azadi March protesters reach Islamabad

Azadi March that was started from Karachi travelled 1450 Km in 5 days to finally reach federal capital. The central rally under the leadership of Maulana Fazlur Rehman had started its journey from Karachi on Sunday.
