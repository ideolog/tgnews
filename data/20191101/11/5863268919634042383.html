PM waives off two requirements for Sikhs coming to Kartarpur

01 Nov 2019, 11:21 by https://www.facebook.com/dunyanews

ISLAMABAD (Dunya News) – Prime Minister (PM) Imran Khan on Friday has announced to waive off two requirements for Sikhs coming to Kartarpur from India.

In a tweet, the premier said that the pilgrims would not need a passport and just a valid Identity Card for traveling to Kartarpur. They also no longer have to register ten days in advance, he continued.
