'9th Gaon Chart Music Awards' reveals 1st round of official nominees!

01 Nov 2019, 11:33 by germainej

The '9th Gaon Chart Music Awards' has revealed the first round of official nominees!

The 'Gaon Chart Music Awards' will be taking place at Seoul Jamsil Indoor Stadium on January 8, 2020, and the award ceremony has revealed the first set of 'Artist of the Year - Digital Music' and 'Artist of the Year - Physical Album' nominees.

Check out the nominee list below!
