#nodevember, the yearly event for everything procedural

01 Nov 2019, 06:30

NodevemberIO writes:

Feel like doing a challenge? #nodevember is all about creating and promoting procedural awesomeness! A whole month to improve and show off your procedural skills.
How to participate:

● Create awesome procedural stuff (inspired by the prompt for each day).
● Post your result, and a screenshot of your node tree on social media.
● Tag your post with #nodevember and #nodevember2019.

Info:

● Anything procedural goes.
● No sculpting or modeling, basic primitives only (plane, cube, sphere), and no image textures.
● You can participate every day, every other day, one day for each of the six categories or just do whatever you feel like.
● This is a challenge, and not a contest. Have fun!

For the latest info go to nodevember.io
Any updates will be posted on the official Twitter account @NodevemberIO
