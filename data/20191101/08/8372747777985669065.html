Two men facing drug charges after Fort Myers drug raid

01 Nov 2019, 08:05 by WFTX Digital Team

FORT MYERS, Fla. -- Two Fort Myers men are now facing drug charges after deputies raided their home Thursday.

The Lee County Sheriff’s Office says they arrested 56-year old Dwayne Johnson and 34-year old Tyrone Samuel.

Deputies say Johnson was selling narcotics and using his home in the 3000 block of Calvin Boulevard as a drug house.

Samuel was wanted on two felony warrants and was found with cocaine. Deputies say they also recovered drug paraphernalia.
